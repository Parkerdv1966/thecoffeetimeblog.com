Twelve days mostly on foot, mostly in Chiado and Alfama. Wrote an entire chapter in a café whose name I never learned. The light at the end of the day was the colour of a flat white, which is now the title of an essay. Came home with three notebooks, a bag of beans from Comoba, and the suspicion that I'd left part of the novel there on purpose.
